ITL Editor

Over the summer, I worked as a research assistant at Inclusive Technology Lab. I worked on the front-end development of a dyslexic-friendly text editor. This application includes multimodal representations of spelling suggestions and text-to-speech features.

IEEE NU Project

This year, I am completing a team project for the IEEE Northwestern Spring Showcase. Our project is a web app that facilitates navigating Northwestern's courses and classrooms.

The iMDIpendent Kit

I worked in a team to develop an "inhaler starter kit" for young children with asthma that helps them use metered-dose inhalers properly and efficiently. This kit included button-activated voice instructions and easy-to-follow instructions with graphics.

The Ascent Alert

I worked in a team to design and 3D-print an electromechanical device that helps students with cerebral palsy safely navigate up the stairs. This device was run by a battery-powered arduino.

Kendama T-Shirt

I designed a t-shirt inspired by the kendama, a traditional Japanese skill toy. This was designed with watercolor, colored pencil, and ink on physical paper, then scanned and created into a shirt design on Photoshop. This product is displayed and sold on Lunatac's website.

Porter T-Shirt

I designed a t-shirt inspired by the song "Goodbye To A World" by Porter Robinson. The design features the lyrics, which repeat over and over again throughout the song, and the iconic "Shelter" icon from the same musical artist. This was designed on Adobe Illustrator and digitally printed on shirts.
